Bio

  • Name : Jonathan Pang
  • Location : Vancouver, B.C.

More commonly known as "Jonno", the editor is best known for his outspokenness, girlish giggle, frequent temper tantrums, and quirky tendency to take off his shirt in any and all occassions.  As the son of a former academic English teacher and a newspaper marketing editor, Jonno holds an incredible disdain for ignorant use of incorrect spelling / grammar / punctuation, indifferent attitudes toward they're and their, and the use of the apostrophe as a pluralization tool.  He has also been know to suffer from aneurysms when exposed to any text presented iN tHiS mAnNeR.

When not yelling at incompetent "business professionals" at his job, Jonno can be found hitting inanimate objects together with Jacob,  getting confrontational with individuals twice his size, and ensuring that car salesmen everywhere realize he knows more about cars and makes more money than them.
