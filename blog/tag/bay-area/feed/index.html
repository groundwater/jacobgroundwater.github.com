I recently took a trip to San Francisco for a week and hung out with Sofia. Some of the things I got to experience:

  1. being scared of Oakland
  2. being scared of richmond (where I stayed)
  3. seeing Mexican workers standing outside of Home Depot (Family Guy jokes make more sense now)
  4. $1.95 iced Americanos (down from $2.58 in BC)
  5. Bay Bridge rush hour traffic
  6. super-sized burritos
  7. light-skinned black people
  8. cheap gas while listening to everyone complain how expensive gas was
  9. non-metric system

In all seriousness, I had a great time. San Francisco is a wonderful city to visit. In fact, I think parts of Vancouver directly copied the vibe of the place (I'm looking at you, Commercial Drive).
